# Transformer

> [!info] Canonical page: [[transformer|Transformer â€” Wiki/Methods/transformer.md]]
> The Transformer is a deep learning model architecture, not a biological entity. Canonical page is under Methods.

The Transformer is a neural network architecture based on self-attention, introduced in "Attention Is All You Need" (Vaswani et al., 2017). In the MLCB course it appears as:

- The backbone of **[[alphafold2|AlphaFold2]]** (Lecture 10) via the [[evoformer|Evoformer]] block
- The architecture underlying **[[esm2|ESM-2]]** and other protein language models (Lecture 11)
- The basis of **[[dnabert|DNABERT]]** and nucleotide-level language models (Lecture 12)

**Why Methods, not Entities:** The Transformer is a computational model architecture, not a biological molecule, gene, or genomic feature.
