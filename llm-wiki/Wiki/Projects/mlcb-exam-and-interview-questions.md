# MLCB Exam And Interview Questions

## Biology Basics
### Q1. Why can the same genome produce many cell types?
Epigenomic regulation and transcription factor programs create different expression states.

## ML Methods
### Q2. Why is representation choice central?
It determines what structure the model can learn.

## Genomics
### Q3. Why are non-coding variants important?
They can disrupt regulatory elements controlling gene expression.

## Protein Modeling
### Q4. Why is attention useful for proteins?
Residues far apart in sequence can interact in 3D.

## Drug Discovery
### Q5. Why use GNNs for molecules?
Molecules are naturally graphs of atoms and bonds.

## Disease Genetics
### Q6. Why is GWAS not mechanism?
It identifies association; mechanism needs causal variant, target gene, cell type, and validation.
